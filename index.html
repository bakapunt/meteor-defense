Meteor Defense

You operate the shield on a spaceship that happened to break down as it entered a meteor field.
Your high-tech shield can absorb any impact it receives. Protect the ship from the meteors.

high score

Tips:

  • Faster moving meteors will do more damage to the ship, but will also award you a higher
    score if absorbed by the shield.
  • The ship crew will repair the ship after each level.
  • As you progress through the meteor field, you may encounter meteors travelling with enough
    energy to destroy your ship with one hit.
